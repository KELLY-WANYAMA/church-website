About Our Church

Discover our history, mission, and leadership

Our History

Founded in 1960, ACK ST JUDES HURUMA began as a small fellowship in Nairobi's Huruma neighborhood. Through God's grace, we've grown into a vibrant congregation serving our community with various ministries and outreach programs.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ and serving our community with love.

Our Vision

To see lives transformed through the Word of God and build a Christ-centered community.

Statement of Faith

  • We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • We uphold the authority of the Bible as God's inspired Word
  • We affirm salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ
  • We practice the sacraments of baptism and holy communion
  • We await the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ
