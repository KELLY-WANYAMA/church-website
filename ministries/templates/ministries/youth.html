Summer Kickoff Pool Party

4:00-8:00 PM

Smith Family Home

About This Event

Join us for an amazing summer kickoff party at the Smith Family Home! We'll have swimming, games, BBQ, worship, and an inspiring message about starting the summer with purpose and faith. This is the perfect opportunity to connect with other youth, make new friends, and create lasting memories.
