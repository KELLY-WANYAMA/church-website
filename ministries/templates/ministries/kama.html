About KAMA

The Kenya Anglican Men's Association (KAMA) is a vital ministry within the Anglican Church of Kenya dedicated to empowering men to become spiritual leaders in their families, churches, and communities.

Founded on biblical principles and Anglican traditions, KAMA provides a platform for men to grow in faith, fellowship, and service while upholding Christian values in society.

Our Anglican Foundation

KAMA operates within the rich tradition of the Anglican Communion, emphasizing:

  • Scriptural authority and tradition
  • Prayer and sacramental life
  • Community and fellowship
  • Social justice and service

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

"To nurture spiritually mature men who transform families and communities through Christ-like leadership and service."

Our Mission

"To empower Anglican men through spiritual growth, fellowship, and service for effective witness in church and society."

Our Motto

"Men of Faith, Men of Action, Men of Service"

The Four Pillars of KAMA

Spiritual Growth

Deepening faith through prayer, Bible study, and sacramental life within the Anglican tradition

Fellowship

Building strong Christian brotherhood and support networks among Anglican men

Service

Serving the church and community through outreach programs and social responsibility

Leadership

Developing men for leadership roles in church, family, and society
