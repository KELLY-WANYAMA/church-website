About KAMA

The Kenya Anglican Men's Association (KAMA) is a vital ministry within the Anglican Church of Kenya dedicated to empowering men to become spiritual leaders in their families, churches, and communities.

Founded on biblical principles and Anglican traditions, KAMA provides a platform for men to grow in faith, fellowship, and service while upholding Christian values in society.

Our Anglican Foundation

KAMA operates within the rich tradition of the Anglican Communion, emphasizing:

  • Scriptural authority and tradition
  • Prayer and sacramental life
  • Community and fellowship
  • Social justice and service
