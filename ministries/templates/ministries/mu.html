About Mother's Union

The Mother's Union is a worldwide Christian organization that seeks to promote the well-being of families worldwide. In the Anglican tradition, we are committed to the support and strengthening of marriage and family life.

Our Anglican Foundation

As part of the Anglican Communion, we uphold the five marks of mission:

  • To proclaim the Good News of the Kingdom
  • To teach, baptize and nurture new believers
  • To respond to human need by loving service
  • To transform unjust structures of society
  • To strive to safeguard the integrity of creation

Our Objectives

Uphold Marriage

To uphold Christ's teaching on the nature of marriage and promote its wider understanding

Family Life

To encourage parents to bring up their children in the faith and life of the Church

Prayer & Worship

To maintain a worldwide fellowship of Christians united in prayer, worship and service

Community Service

To promote conditions in society favorable to stable family life and the protection of children

Mother's Union Prayer

"Loving Lord, we thank you for your love so freely given to us all. We pray for families around the world. - The Mother's Union Prayer
